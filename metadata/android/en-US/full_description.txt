MyGrades shows you the grades directly from your univertiy grading system. It simulates a Web-Browser to spare you the tedious job of clicking through non optimized university web platforms. Furthermore it stores your marks locally so they are alwaysat hand. The login details are securly stored only on your smartphone.

<b>Permissions</b>

<i>Run at startup and Deactivate Hibernation</i>
Will be needed in order to periodically check if a new grade was listed. (only if you activate it in the settings).

<i>Control vibtrating alter</i>
If MyGrades detects that you`ve got a new mark, we will let you know.

<i>Network Connectivity</i>
Where else should we get your grades from? ;)
It is also needed in order to show you if you are connected to the internet.
